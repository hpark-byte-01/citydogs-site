Support

Contact Us

For questions, feedback, or issues with CityDogs, please email us at citydogs.info@gmail.com.

Report a Problem

If you're experiencing a bug or technical issue, please include a description of the problem and the device you're using. Screenshots are helpful.

Report a User

You can report users directly within the app by visiting their profile and tapping "Report." You can also email us with details and we will investigate promptly.

Account Deletion

To delete your account, go to Profile > Settings > Account > Delete Account in the app. Your account will be scheduled for permanent deletion after a 21-day grace period. You can cancel the deletion by logging back in during this period.
